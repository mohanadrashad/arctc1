About Us

  • Art of Arkan Al-Tameer is a company established in 2009 as one of the leading companies in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA). It carries out its investment activities in various fields such as technology, mobile accessories, electronics, clothing, perfumes and cosmetics, which contribute to supplying the Saudi market with the state o the art of the global markets relevant products. The company aims to continue its strategic plan by expanding its activities in various investment fields, such as restaurants and cafes, with high quality in line with the Kingdom's Vision 2030.

  • The successes achieved by (Art of Arkan Al-Taameer) and the increase in demand for its products, which cover the kingdom's largest commercial malls, is due to the company's reliance mainly on the quality of its products, Qualified staff, experience record in marketing, that meet the needs and increasing demands among customers as well as the company's interest of its consultant's experiences

  • The company believes that products quality and qualifications and experiences of the consultants and employees are the source of the company's success in the future
